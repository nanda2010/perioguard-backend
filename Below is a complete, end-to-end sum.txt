# **PerioGuard AI**

### *AI-Assisted Early Detection and Continuous Monitoring of Peri-Implant Diseases*

---

## **1. Project Overview**

PerioGuard AI is a **doctor-guided, AI-assisted dental diagnostic platform** designed for the **early detection, risk assessment, and continuous monitoring of peri-implant diseases**, including **peri-implant mucositis and peri-implantitis**.

Unlike patient-only diagnostic apps, PerioGuard AI is built around **clinical supervision**, ensuring that **all AI outputs support—but never replace—professional dental decision-making**.

The system integrates:

* Patient health history
* Radiographic image analysis
* AI-based risk scoring
* Doctor verification
* Automated follow-up reminders

This approach ensures **accuracy, accountability, safety, and real-world clinical relevance**.

---

## **2. Medical Motivation & Problem Statement**

Peri-implant diseases often:

* Progress **silently**
* Show **minimal early symptoms**
* Are detected **late** through subjective interpretation of radiographs

Current challenges include:

* Manual radiographic interpretation variability
* Delayed diagnosis
* Poor patient follow-up compliance
* Lack of supervised digital diagnostic tools

There is a strong clinical need for a **structured, AI-assisted, doctor-controlled system** that enables:

* Early risk identification
* Standardized evaluation
* Continuous patient monitoring

---

## **3. Core Design Philosophy**

### 🔹 Doctor-First Architecture

* No patient can use the system without doctor authorization
* AI outputs are **decision-support**, not diagnoses
* Doctors retain final clinical responsibility

### 🔹 Medical Safety & Ethics

* Prevents self-diagnosis
* Ensures traceability
* Maintains patient-doctor accountability
* Designed for future regulatory compliance (ISO / medical AI standards)

---

## **4. System Architecture Overview**

The system is divided into **three major layers**:

### **A. Backend (Flask-Based Medical Logic Layer)**

Handles:

* Doctor and patient management
* Data validation
* Radiograph handling
* AI analysis logic
* Reminder scheduling

### **B. AI Analysis Layer**

Processes:

* Dental radiographs (X-ray / OPG / CBCT slices)
* Risk factor integration
* Disease probability scoring

### **C. Android Application (User Interface Layer)**

Provides:

* Doctor panel
* Patient panel
* Secure data upload
* Report visualization
* Notifications

---

## **5. User Roles & Workflow**

### **5.1 Doctor Panel (Clinical Control Layer)**

Doctors can:

* Register/login securely
* Create unique **Patient IDs**
* Assign patient metadata (age, implant site, etc.)
* Review AI-generated analysis
* Add clinical notes
* Finalize diagnosis
* Monitor patient compliance

> **Key Rule:**
> Patients **cannot access the app independently**.

---

### **5.2 Patient Panel (Controlled Access)**

Patients:

* Login using **doctor-generated Patient ID**
* Upload:

  * Medical history (diabetes, smoking, medications)
  * Dental history
  * Implant details
  * Symptoms
* Upload radiographic images
* View AI-assisted reports (read-only)

---

## **6. Backend Implementation (Current Stage)**

The backend is implemented using **Python + Flask** and includes:

### **Implemented Modules**

✔ Doctor creates patient
✔ Patient ID generation
✔ Patient health data storage
✔ Radiograph upload and secure storage
✔ Patient-doctor data linking

Data is currently stored in **temporary in-memory structures**, with future migration planned to a secure database.

---

## **7. Radiographic Image Handling**

The system supports:

* Intra-oral X-rays
* OPG images
* CBCT slices (optional)

Workflow:

1. Image uploaded by patient or doctor
2. Stored securely on the server
3. Linked to Patient ID
4. Passed to AI analysis module

---

## **8. AI-Assisted Diagnostic Logic**

The AI module performs:

* Image preprocessing
* Feature extraction
* Risk estimation

### **AI Outputs Include:**

* Gum inflammation detection
* Bone loss severity estimation (mild/moderate/severe)
* Implant stability risk
* Early peri-implantitis indicators
* Weak tooth support warnings

⚠️ **Important:**
AI does **not** provide final diagnosis.
Doctor confirmation is mandatory.

---

## **9. Automated Follow-Up Reminder System (15-Day Cycle)**

To ensure continuous monitoring:

* Follow-up reminders are generated every **15 days**
* Based on last visit or last radiograph upload
* Reminders sent via:

  * In-app notifications
  * SMS / Email (future enhancement)

### Missed Follow-Up Handling:

* Patient flagged in Doctor Panel
* Doctor intervention enabled early

This improves:

* Patient compliance
* Early disease detection
* Implant survival rates

---

## **10. Android Application Role**

### **Why Android Studio Is Used Later**

* Backend logic is built and validated first
* Android app acts as **interface only**
* Reduces errors and confusion

### Android App Responsibilities:

* Doctor login UI
* Patient login via ID
* Image selection and upload
* Display AI results
* Show reminders and alerts
